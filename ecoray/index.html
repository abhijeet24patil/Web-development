About Us

Have you ever thought of renting the sun? Well, you heard it right; Ecoray does it! Yes, we rent the sun in the form of solar equipments to the families; whose children could not study at the Night because of frequent power cuts, to the farmer who cannot afford to walk 20kms a day to get his seed dried, and to those people who breathe the carbon emissions from kerosene lamps instead of fresh air. We at Ecoray aim at providing the affordable solar solutions to the fragmented income groups.Solar power is the last energy resource that isn’t owned yet- nobody taxes yet, as rightly Said by Dan Quayle “Its time for the human race to enter the Solar System”.

Vision

To replace the conventional source of electricity with the renewable source energy.

Mission

To continuously provide the low cost innovative solar solutions & services to meet the daily electricity needs of the fragmented income groups.

Our Approach

To reach entire population of the village, it’s very important to connect to the each person of the village. In order to reach every corner of the village, we started by approaching the village punchayats, head of cooperative society, village forest committees and other well known personalities of the village, who act as opinion leaders to the residents. This helped us in creating the awareness of the service and use of solar appliances.

The main beneficiaries were those who could not afford to the solar products as they are very expensive, this category include the below poverty people such as daily wage workers, the farmers and many others who rely on their daily income for their living. The absence of electricity grid has not only impacted the lives of adults, but also that of the children, they were depending kerosene lamps as source of light, which also leads to the respiratory hazards. Replacing the kerosene lamps with solar lanterns not only prevent the respiratory problems but also leads to the greener and safer environment. To install the charging points at the accessible centers, we associated with cooperative societies, village forest committees and Village Punchayats. This association helped us to provide the solar energy as alternative source of electricity without the presence of grid. These communities provided us the space and carried out the marketing activities. The lanterns were charged at these installation centers and the same was provided to the target audience. Solar panels were provided along with the lanterns if they opt for the packages above 3 or more days. This also led to the employment generation, were in a field manager was trained on how to do the first level repairs, and he was also responsible to make sure that he get back the lanterns if the customer does not turn up on time.

How this actually changed the lives of people?

Ecoray’s service was able to:
  • Change the perception of solar products from premium products to low cost affordable.
  • Reduce in the use of Kerosene and dry cell batteries by the use of Solar Lanterns, hence making the environment green & safe.
  • Generation of employment
  • Increase the number of children going to school

How it all Started

Sometimes its the journey that teaches you a lot about your destination.

  • Fun Ride to Social Idea

    It all started with the friends going for ride to uttara kannada District, they visited Vanalli while on the way to shivagange falls. Being impressed by the people of the village, they decided to spend a day more. On the particular night, there was power cut. They saw the children struggling to study in the light Kerosene lamps. They came up with the idea of renting the solar equipment, named as Igniting minds.

  • Meeting the Mentor

    An idea without a proper direction is like a bird without the wings, they met the Enthusiast Kishor Jagirdar the prime motivator, who helped them to convert idea into reality.

  • Idea to a Reality

    Many brilliant ideas fail, because of the flaws in the execution. Hence an idea to be reality, its very important to understand how this idea can best fitted into the needs of the target audience. It all began with the aggressive research to capture the need of the rural people.

  • Birth of Ecoray

    Ecoray started with its various offerings that caters the electricity needs of the rural people. “Because Sun Shines Equally on Every house”
